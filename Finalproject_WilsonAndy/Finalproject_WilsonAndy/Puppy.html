Trainning

Puppys are babies, they are not come out of the box ready. Having a basic start of dog behavior will lead to a well balanced dog in a 2 1/2 year time. Allowing a firm but caring nature that will allow growth and trust for the owner. Exposing to different people and enviorment is key for a well socail dog. Yet the dogs breed might have a protective nature.

Consistency

Trainning is everyday, when time allows during feeding, or exersize and play allowing the puppy know boundries and not allowing to get away with little things will help it be a obedent dog. Having time throughout it's life with exersize.


Good start


  1. Potty/Crate
  2. Sit/Lay
  3. Heel/Stay
  4. Release
